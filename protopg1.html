On this website we will be comparing Sustainble Development Goal #7: Affordable and Clean Energy and Sustainable Development Goal #11: Sustainable Cities and Communities. If you don't know, sustainable development goals, otherwise known as SDGs are 'are a collection of 17 interlinked global goals designed to be a blueprint to achieve a better and more sustainable future for all'. As mentioned, this website will draw the comparison between SDGs 7 and 11. The purpose behind comparing these two goals is to show how they are related and to make others more aware of both these prevalent issues. Have fun on the website!

SDG #7 - Affordable & Clean Energy

SDG #7, Affordable and Clean Energy. This Sustainable

SDG #11 - Sustainable Cities & Communities

SDG #11, Sustainable Cities and Communities. This

Sustainable Development Goal seeks to make cities and

human settlements inclusive, safe, resilient and sustainable.
